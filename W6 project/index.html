South Patagonia

Perito Moreno Glacier

One of the most active glaciers in the world, declared a World Heritage Site. Its imposing blue ice walls advance towards Lake Argentino, creating spectacular calving events.

A country of extraordinary contrasts

Argentina is one of the countries with the greatest landscape diversity in the world. In its vast territory, the subtropical jungle of the Northeast, the Patagonian steppe of the South, the Andean deserts of the Northwest, and the fertile valleys of Cuyo coexist.

Each region offers unique experiences for the traveler: trekking on ancient ice, contemplating geological formations millions of years old, sighting native wildlife, and much more.
